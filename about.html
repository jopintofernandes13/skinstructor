About Jo

I began my life on snow in a competitive ski racing environment—learning the importance of precision, discipline and respect for the mountain. Over time, that competitive spirit evolved into a vocation for teaching. I love helping people discover confidence on skis, and I learn from every student I teach.

My approach blends technical coaching with playful, confidence-building learning. Whether you're aiming to refine carving technique, tackle powder, or explore new terrain, I tailor every session to your goals.

Philosophy

Teaching is a conversation. I bring curiosity and attentiveness—every client teaches me something new about the mountain, technique, and culture. That mutual exchange makes every lesson rewarding.
